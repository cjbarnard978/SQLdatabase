Tak ve same furth of Kirkealdie At Leith the xviii day of Januare 1571 The Commissionaris of ye kirk for certane ressonable canssis and consideratiounes movemg thame Ordeanes thare collectour and elerk ouer the rentallis to exchange of yair assumptioune of ye beire of ye abbay of Dumfermling yj. ch. viij.b. beir quhilk wes takin out of befuir of ye parochin of Kinglassie To be now takin out of ye ferme beir of ye nedder grange of Kinghorne geveand be zeir vj. ch. And of ve dry multouris of wester Kinghorne geveand be zeir viij hb. And sua Kinglassie qnhitk payit in the Kirkis assumptioune of befoir x . ch . ij. b,j. f. sall now pay bot iij . clr. x.b.}. pe. and the remanent of ye beir quhilk wes assumeit ont of Kinglassie of befoir extending to ye saidis vj. ch. vii}. b. to be now tane ont of wester Kinghorne and dry multeris befoir specifeit Makand in the haill x. ch .ij.b.i.p. quhilk wes tane haillalie of Kinglassie of befoir Provyding alwayis give ye Kirk findis thame selffs hurt be this exchange throw evill pav' or wtherwayis that thay and thair col- lectour sall hawe recours gane to thair awld assumptioun or ells the commendatour of Dumfermling sall assigne thame better pay' at vair optieun quhil vai find thame selffis in als guid cais as thay wer be thair awld assumptioun .
