1567.) RENTALL OF THE ABBACIE, Sig

Iffobell lilie halfe ane land -

Thomas lilic halfe land -

Hendrie veycht halfe land -

Johne haiftie thrie halfe landis -

James Roull halfe ane land -

Georg hog ane land and tua cot landis -

Robene haiftie ane land .

Robene brig v quarteris of ane land -

Williame myller halfe ane land and tua cot landis -
Mychell lychten vij quarteris of land -

James bald halfe ane land and tua cot landis -
Willie ftenfone thre halfe landis -

Mertene bonattyne thre halfe landis -

Jennet fuanttone thre halfe landis -

Walter ellot ane quarter of ane land -

Jok fieill halfe ane land -

George glendung ane cot land -

Robene hog halfe ane land -

George hog v quarteris of land and halfe ane coit land
Stenie bonattyne halfe ane land -

James veir halfe ane land -

James fienouf ane quarter land -

Beflie ellot ane quarter of anc land -

Dand vilfone ane hufhand land -

James miller ane eoit land -

George hog fone to James hog halfe ane land -
James hog halfe ane land -

Ifobell kar halfe ane land and ane halfe coit laud -
f land and tua coit landis -

Nyckoll renattoune vij quarteris
Jok fteill ane quarter of land -
Beflie ellot the halfe of the mill -
